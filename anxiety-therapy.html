Anxiety Therapy

Anxiety therapy for overthinking, fear and feeling constantly on edge.

Anxiety can make life feel smaller. It may show up as panic, avoidance, overthinking, tension, people-pleasing or a constant sense that something is wrong. Therapy can help you understand what anxiety is protecting you from and how to respond differently.
