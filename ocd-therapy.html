Frequently asked questions about OCD therapy

What is OCD?

OCD involves intrusive thoughts, images, urges or doubts, followed by compulsions that try to reduce anxiety or create certainty.

What are mental compulsions?

Mental compulsions can include rumination, checking feelings, reviewing memories, analysing thoughts, neutralising images or trying to feel certain.

Do you work with Pure O?

Yes. Pure O often involves hidden compulsions rather than obvious external rituals. Therapy can help identify and reduce these patterns.

Do you offer OCD therapy online?

Yes. I offer online therapy across the UK and in-person therapy in London Bridge.
